About ZIAS

ZIAS is a collective born out of the steadily developed aspirations of two brothers in the hope of carrying the ideological brilliance behind design and balance for the growth and development of public and private infrastructure in Africa and the world. We have been able to bring forth immaculate designs with the joint expertise of Architecture and Structural Engineering. We strive to bring the next new and well-built craft that addresses the interests of our clients while considering the environment.

Construction Supervision

Our team of experts is trained to provide strategic supervision, making sure the workflow and interests of the client go side by side with the desired quality, inclusive of technological updates, considering the safety of lives and durability of the structure.



Structural Engineering

Aiming to build structures that can pass through generations while resisting environmental conditions and human use, we work to design structures that can withstand stress and pressure.




Civil Engineeringn

This is one of the areas of expertise in constructing and maintaining infrastructures, bringing to reality a community with striking designs and empirical constructions based on our team's skillfulness. ZIAS is expanding the Civil Engineering service to include the design of highways and railroads.
